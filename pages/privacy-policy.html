Effective Date

2025/01/01

Introduction

At Encrypti0n.com (accessible from https://encrypti0n.com ), we prioritize your privacy and are committed to providing a secure experience. This Privacy Policy outlines the practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection. Please read this policy carefully.

Data Collection

Encrypti0n.com does not collect, store, or process any personal information, including but not limited to IP addresses, names, addresses, or contact details. We strive to ensure complete user anonymity.

Cookies

Encrypti0n.com does not use cookies to track or monitor user behavior.

Third Parties

No third parties are involved in the operation of this website. Encrypti0n.com functions independently and does not share or disclose user information with any external entities.

All data entered on Encrypti0n.com is processed locally on your device. No entered data is transmitted from your device to any server, ensuring complete privacy and control over your input.
